## Additional Features to Add

### 1. Maintenance Mode (Temporary Blockage)

Admin can mark a car as:

```text
Available
Booked
Under Maintenance
Inactive
```

If a car is under maintenance:

```text
This vehicle is temporarily unavailable due to scheduled maintenance.
Please choose another vehicle or contact us for assistance.
```

The car will not appear in booking results.

---

### 2. Dynamic Business Settings

Fields:

```text
Business Name

Instagram Link
```

Database:

### 3. Dynamic Logo

Instead of hardcoding a logo:

```text
[Upload Logo]
```

Upload through Cloudinary.

Wherever logo appears:

```jsx
<img src={settings.logoUrl} />
```

Changing the logo once updates it across the entire website.

---

### 4. Dynamic Business Name

Instead of:

```text
ABC Rental Services
```

Use:

```jsx
{settings.businessName}
```

Examples:

```text
Decide later:
DriveEasy Rentals

Later update:
Premium Wheels Rentals

Later update:
XYZ Car Rental Services
```

No code changes required.
